ℹ️ Help & User Guide – HealthRisk AI

About HealthRisk AI

HealthRisk AI is a machine learning-powered platform that helps patients and doctors predict the risk of multiple diseases based on medical inputs. It currently supports:

  • 🩸 Diabetes Risk Estimation
  • ❤️ Heart Disease Risk Estimation
  • 🧠 Parkinson’s Disease Risk Estimation

How to Use the Website

  1. Navigate to the disease you want to test from the Navigation Bar.
  2. Fill out the input form with your medical details (as shown below).
  3. Click on the Predict button.
  4. View the result showing whether you are at high risk or low risk.
  5. You can also use the Chatbot for quick help and FAQs.

🩸 Diabetes Prediction Parameters

Parameter Description
Pregnancies Number of times pregnant (0 if male).
Glucose Plasma glucose concentration after 2 hours in an oral glucose test.
Blood Pressure Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg).
Skin Thickness Triceps skin fold thickness (mm).
Insulin 2-hour serum insulin (mu U/ml).
BMI Body Mass Index = weight (kg) / height² (m²).
Diabetes Pedigree Function Likelihood of diabetes based on family history.
Age Age in years.

❤️ Heart Disease Prediction Parameters

Parameter Description
Age Patient's age in years.
Sex 1 = Male, 0 = Female.
Chest Pain Type (cp) 0–3 categories of chest pain experienced.
Resting Blood Pressure Blood pressure at rest (mm Hg).
Cholesterol Serum cholesterol in mg/dl.
Fasting Blood Sugar (fbs) 1 = >120 mg/dl, 0 = ≤120 mg/dl.
Rest ECG Electrocardiographic results (0–2).
Max Heart Rate (thalach) Maximum heart rate achieved.
Exercise Induced Angina (exang) 1 = Yes, 0 = No.
Oldpeak ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.
Slope Slope of the ST segment (0–2).
CA Number of major vessels (0–3) colored by fluoroscopy.
Thal Thalassemia value (0–3).

🧠 Parkinson's Prediction Parameters

Parameter Description
Fo (Fundamental Frequency) Average vocal pitch in Hz.
Fhi Maximum vocal pitch detected.
Flo Minimum vocal pitch detected.
Jitter Variation in frequency (voice stability).
Shimmer Variation in amplitude (voice strength).
